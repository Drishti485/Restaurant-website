About Us

 

La Vista opened in 1996 is located on the Ashvem Road at the buzzing end of the Ashvem Beach.In the winter La Vista restaurant is cozy, comfy and friendly. With the outdoor seating you will find La Vista in the summer breezy, bright and great fun!

The cuisine in Spain is as important for the conversation and company as for the delicious food. Spaniards love the opportunity to be with friends, family, and meet strangers over a bite to eat.

We hope you enjoy our little bit of Spain at La Vista restaurant right here in Goa.
